INDUSTRIES

Nagarro is an enterprise solutions provider, enabling industrial transformation with strategy and design solutions. We leverage our expertise across multiple industries and business functions to drive technology-led business breakthroughs.

AUTOMATIVE

Global economic shifts, changing consumer preferences and pressing sustainability needs present new challenges and opportunities to the automotive industry. Autonomous mobility, digitalization and electrification are offering new ways to create value for businesses, consumers and society.

BANKING AND INSURANCE

While banking, insurance, and financial services are being redefined by major disruptions, traditional methods of customer acquisition, engagement and retention are also evolving rapidly. This means a massive overhaul of existing business models is imminent and innovation is key to handling this change.

ENERGY AND UTILITIES

Changing economics, stricter regulations, and disruptive innovations - all this in a highly capital intensive context. This industry that powers the activities of the human race is itself rather powerless in the face of some complex challenges.

TELECOMMUNICATIONS

The key challenge in front of a telecom operator today is not how to better connect its customers, but how to better connect with its customers. With data trumping voice, the consumers are associating their experience with the device manufacturers and application providers, relegating the telecom operators to a status of a "fat, dumb pipe".
